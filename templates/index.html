About software

The neonatal period begins at birth and includes the first month of life. Although this period is a highly vulnerable time as infants complete the many physiologic adjustments required for extrauterine existence, this transition is uneventful for some newborns. Since neonate's diseases, unlike adults, have the same signs and symptoms, making them more difficult to diagnose. That is why neonatal diseases are paramount in medicine. Besides, ruling out the differential diagnosis at this age is very critical. On the other hand, diseases might have many overlaps, and pediatricians rely on their memory and experience. Therefore, designing software in this field helps physicians and pediatricians to recall different diagnoses. This software has attempted to make diagnoses easier for physicians by limiting diagnoses based on clinical symptoms. In addition, attempts have been made to include key symptoms in each disease to differentiate between diagnoses easier.
